MF DOOM: Fillet-O-Rapper

Beer battered cod, twice-fried fries, remoulade, hot sauce, & American cheese on a potato roll. This is part of the Cooking The Album project for MF DOOM's MM..FOOD made by Frank Davis

Ingredients

FILLET-O-RAPPER

The following portions make 4 servings/sandwiches

  • 4 portions of Beer Battered Fish & Chips (see recipe below)
  • 4 potato rolls
  • 8 slices American cheese
  • Remoulade (see recipe below)

Fish & Chips

  • 2 medium russet potatoes
  • 4 medium cod or haddock fillets (about 6-ounces each)
  • 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1/2 cup Ale or Lager beer very cold (you may need a little more)
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

Remoulade

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce (Red Devil is preferable)
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced fine
  • 1 scallions, whites & greens diced fine
  • 1 tablespoon Rock Candy Jalapenos, minced fine
  • 1 teaspoon Rock Candy Jalapeno juice
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Directions

FILLET-O-RAPPER

  1. Begin by making the fish & chips and remoulade recipes below.
  2. Spread a tablespoon of remoulade on the bottom bun and top with a slice of cheese to assemble the sandwiches
  3. Add a few fries, one piece of cod, and a few more fries
  4. lace another slice of cheese over the fries on top, drizzle a tablespoon or so of remoulade on the cheese, a couple of dashes of hot sauce, and finish with the top bun
  5. Serve any leftover fries on the side
  6. Repeat same steps for the other sandwiches

Fish & Chips

Preparation

  1. Scrub the potatoes well, then cut into 1/4″ thick batons
  2. Dry thoroughly with paper towels and leave them sitting on paper towels to allow the surface of the potatoes to dry out for about 30 minutes
  3. You can skip above step if you're pressed for time, but your potatoes won't turn out as crisp
  4. Mix the flour, cornstarch, paprika, and onion powder in a medium bowl until well combined
  5. Dust each fish fillet with the flour mixture on all sides

Cooking

Chips
  1. Add 1 1/2″ of vegetable oil to a heavy-bottomed pot and heat to 330°F
  2. Line a 2 wire racks with 2 layers of paper towels each
  3. Fry the potatoes in batches until a light tan color and the edges are just starting to brown
  4. Transfer the fried chips to one prepared rack to drain
  5. When the potatoes are done frying, add the baking powder to the flour mixture and whisk together
  6. It's okay if there are still a few lumps, just make sure you do not overmix the batter or it will end up heavy
Fish
  1. Using the same oil, dip the fillets in the same batter and fry them in batches.
  2. Flip the fillets over with tongs when you see the edges start to turn light brown
  3. Transfer to the second prepared rack as they finish frying
Second Frying
  1. When the fish is done frying, increase the heat of the oil to 375 degrees
  2. Fry the chips a second time until they are golden brown and crisp
  3. Drain on a rack and sprinkle with salt
  4. Repeat same step for the fish, serve the fish and chips with lemon wedges or vinegar

Remoulade

  1. Place all ingredients on a medium bowl
  2. Whisk thoroughly to combine thoroughly
  3. Store in the refrigerator
