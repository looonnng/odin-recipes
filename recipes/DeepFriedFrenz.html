MF DOOM: Deep Fried Frenz

Deep-fried grilled cheese & tomato soup dumplings. This is part of Frank Davis' Cooking The Album project for MF DOOM's MM..FOOD

Ingredients

The following recipe will make 24 dumplings:

  • 12-ounces of homemade tomato soup or condensed tomato soup - do not add water or milk
  • 12 slices yellow Kraft Singles American cheese
  • 24 slices of Wonder Bread, edges cut off
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil

Directions

Preparing The Dumplings

  1. Fill an ice cube tray with 1 tablespoon of soup (should have enough soup for 12 molds)
  2. Freeze for at least 6 hours or overnight
  3. Cut each cube in half after freezing and wrap each piece in half a slice of cheese
  4. Place the cheese-wrapped cube diagonal on a piece of bread
  5. Moisten your hand in the water wrap the bread around the cheese
  6. Flip the bread package over so the seams are in the moistened palm of your hand and press into a ball
  7. Make sure there are no open seams

Cooking The Dumplings

  1. Heat oil to 325â„‰ in a deep fryer, a deep, heavy-bottomed pot, or a cast-iron skillet
  2. Skillet frying is preferable because it creates a nice browning on the dumplings
  3. Fry dumplings in batches until golden brown, 5-6 minutes,and let dry on paper towels
  4. Wait 5 minutes before eating because trust me this step is important
